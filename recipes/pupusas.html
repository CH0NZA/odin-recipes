Pupusas Recipe

This is a common Salvadoran dish. This is not a complete recipe. This is meant to fulfill the requirements of the project.

Ingredients

  • Masa
  • Queso
  • Loroco
  • Frijoles

Steps

  1. Refry refried beans until the consistency is similar to play doh
  2. Blend Loroco until it is a fine
  3. Mix blended Loroco into your cheese
  4. Oil your hand to prevent sticking
  5. Grab some of your masa and stuff it with your queso-Loroco mix with some of your frijoles
  6. Play with the stuffed ball of masa and flatten to a quarter-inch disk
  7. Oil flat top pan
  8. flip in 8-minutes
